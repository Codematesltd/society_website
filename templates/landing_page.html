Organization Savings Rules

  • Loan for members: Loans will be given to members at an interest rate of 10% (simple interest).
  • Organization savings: Members should save a minimum of ₹5 per month in the organization’s savings.
  • Pigmy deposits: Daily collection savings (pigmy) scheme is available.
  • Fixed Deposits (FD): FD interest rate is 9% annually.
  • Educational assistance: Children of members studying SSLC (10th grade) or higher will receive educational assistance. (C.G.) will decide the assistance amount. From 2001 onwards, the amount is ₹150 per student; maximum limit is ₹1000 per family.
  • Death fund: In case of the death of a member, a relief amount of ₹500/- will be provided to the family.
